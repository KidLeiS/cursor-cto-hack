# Hackathon Collaboration
- Assume multiple agents and people are working concurrently on `main`.
- Treat existing uncommitted changes as someone else’s work; preserve them exactly.
- Stay within the assigned frontend or backend area and avoid unrelated files.
- Re-read files immediately before editing because they may have changed.
- Never switch branches, stash, reset, clean, revert, or overwrite others’ work.
- Do not stop shared processes or change global environment/configuration.
- Avoid broad formatting, dependency updates, and migrations unless explicitly requested.
- Coordinate before changing shared contracts, schemas, APIs, or cross-stack files.
- Run scoped checks and stage only your own files; report conflicts instead of resolving destructively.
